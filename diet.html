Penguin Diet

Penguins feed primarily on seafood depending on the species and habitat. Their diet includes:

  • Fish: Small varieties like anchovies, sardines, and mackerel.
  • Squid: Some species of penguins, especially the larger ones, consume squid.
  • Krill: A staple in the diet of many penguins, especially in the Antarctic regions.
